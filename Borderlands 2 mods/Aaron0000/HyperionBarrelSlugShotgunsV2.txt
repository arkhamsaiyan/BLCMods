By Aaron0000

Changes the Hyperion shotgun barrel into a slug-firing shotgun. Modifies all normal shotguns and the Striker into slug-firing shotguns (Does not turn the Conference Call, Heartbreaker, or Interfacer into slug-shotguns).

NOTE;
Vertical Grip accessory (Carnage's included) no longer adds two projectiles to help keep the Hyperion barrel balanced. Certain shotguns have been granted two additional projectiles as compensation (disable as needed from the bottom section if using other mods that use said weapon);
