########

By Aaron0000


Turns the Octo into an interpretation of the Laser Disker (within reason and limitations) in the form of a Splat Gun.

Features;

Always explosive element (although it does not erase elemental versions).
Should not spawn with a Vertical Grip (although it does not erase versions with Vertical Grips).
Fires a swiftly-moving blob that explodes on contact with walls or enemies. 
Very high damage, low rate of fire. 
Does not have the bonus airborne damage.

As a bonus, this mod also makes all standard Splat Guns get Grenade Damage bonuses in order to give the Laser Disker grenade bonuses.
########
