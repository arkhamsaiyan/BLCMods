By Aaron0000

Turns the CHOPPER into a weapon inspired by the Heavy's weapon of choice.

Features;
Sizable damage increase.
Ridiculous fire rate.
Very large magazine size.
Wider bullet spread.
Not very effective ADS.
Slow minigun spin-up.
Increased damage resistance when wielded.
Movement speed penalty when wielded.

NOTES;
The Chopper doesn't actually have a unique material I can use to alter the visuals, so I altered the Evil Smasher's. Disable those lines if you don't want the Evil Smasher's visuals to be altered, and don't mind the old visuals of the CHOPPER.
If using S&S Forever, there is a fix for it at the bottom of the file. Activate this fix after S&S Forever is activated.

########
